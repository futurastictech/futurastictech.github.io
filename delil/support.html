❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Are my read cards saved?

Yes, all reading data is stored locally on your device. Your progress is preserved even when the app is closed.

How does the search feature work?

Tap the Search tab in the bottom menu. Your query is searched simultaneously across all modules (Proofs, Miracles, Responses, Quotes).

Does the app work offline?

Yes. All content is loaded onto your device and requires no internet connection.

How many proofs are there?

The app contains 117+ proof cards. Combined with the Miracles, Responses and Quotes modules, the total content count is much higher.

The app is crashing — what should I do?

Try fully closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, uninstall and reinstall it. If you still experience problems, please contact us.

Is DELÎL free?

Yes, DELÎL is completely free.
