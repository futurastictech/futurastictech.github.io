Getting Started

Tap Start the Test on the home screen and answer the short quiz. Perfumella then suggests three perfumes matched to your taste. You can also browse the Top 100, Celebrity Perfumes, the Daily Perfume, or blend scents in Mix Perfumes.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do I need an account? No. Perfumella works without sign-up; nothing is stored on our servers.
  • How do I switch language? Open Settings (gear icon) and choose English or Turkish.
  • Why do I see ads? Ads keep the app free. They are served by Google AdMob.
  • A perfume is missing or has wrong data. The catalogue is large and updated over time — let us know and we’ll review it.
  • How does “Mix Perfumes” work? Pick 2–3 perfumes; the app combines their notes and accords and reports how the blend would smell.
