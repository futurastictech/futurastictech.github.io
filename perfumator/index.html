What is Perfumator?

Perfumator is a bilingual (English / Turkish) fragrance companion. Answer a few playful questions and get three tailored perfume recommendations from a catalogue of nearly 40,000 perfumes — your strongest match, a safer crowd-pleaser, and a bolder, more original pick.

Features

🧬 Scent Quiz

A short, data-driven quiz maps your taste to your fragrance DNA.

🔮 Top 100

Yearly best-of lists by gender, from 1977 to today.

⭐ Celebrity Perfumes

See which scents your favourite celebrities wear.

💊 Mix Perfumes

Blend 2–3 perfumes and get a detailed report of how the mix would smell.

📅 Daily Perfume

A fresh suggestion every day, with how-to-wear tips.

🔍 Search & Twin

Search the full catalogue and find your perfume’s closest twin.
