❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Are my scores saved?

Yes, all your scores, level and settings are stored locally on your device. They are preserved even when the app is closed.

How are the SnapIQ score and cognitive age calculated?

They are computed from a weighted average of the 29 tests. This is purely for entertainment and is not a medical or scientific diagnosis.

How do I change the language?

You choose a language on first launch; later you can switch between Turkish and English from Settings.

Why do I see ads?

The app is free and supported by Google AdMob ads. On iOS we ask for tracking permission; if you decline, you only see non-personalized ads.

The app is crashing or freezing — what should I do?

Try fully closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, uninstall and reinstall it. If you still experience problems, please contact us.

Is SnapIQ free?

Yes, SnapIQ is completely free.
